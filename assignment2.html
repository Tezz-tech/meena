BOOK OVERVIEW

"The Twisted Series" is a collection of gripping novels that combines elements of romance, suspense, and mystery to deliver an unforgettable reading experience. With masterful storytelling and intricate character development, Ana Huang weaves a web of intrigue that will leave readers craving for more.

TWISTED LOVE

The story is an opposites-attract relationship between Ava, an upbeat Chinese college student and her brother's best friend, a controlling, possessive antihero with trust issues.

TWISTED GAMES

Regal, strong-willed, and bound by the chains of duty, Princess Bridget dreams of the freedom to live and love as she chooses. But when her brother abdicates, she's suddenly faced with the prospect of a loveless, politically expedient marriage and a throne she never wanted.

TWISTED HATE

Told from alternating first-person perspectives, Twisted Hate follows the hate-to-love romance between Jules Ambrose and Josh Chen. Jules and Josh both navigate dark pasts, betrayal, and the vulnerability that comes with committed relationships.

TWISTED LIES

A fake-dating billionaire romance from New York Times bestselling author and BookTok sensation Ana Huang. Charming, deadly, and smart enough to hide it, Christian Harper is a monster dressed in the perfectly tailored suits of a gentleman.

How the books are Connected

The series is part of the Ana Huang Universe where all the books are interconnected standalones except for the If Love Duet. Chronologically the Ana Huang Universe starts with the If Love series, and it's followed by the Twisted and the Kings of Sin series.
